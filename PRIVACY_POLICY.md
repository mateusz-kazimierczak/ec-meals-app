# Privacy Policy for EC Meals App

**Last Updated:** October 4, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how EC Meals App ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** First name and user identification when you create an account
- **Meal Preferences:** Your meal selections and dietary preferences
- **Authentication Data:** Login credentials used to access your account

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, and app version
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the app, including meal selections and preferences updates

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain meal ordering functionality
- Process and manage your meal selections
- Send notifications about meal deadlines and updates
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Authenticate and secure your account

## Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications to:
- Remind you about meal selection deadlines
- Notify you of important updates to the meal service
- Alert you about changes to your meal preferences

You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using Firebase services
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
- Meal selections are stored on secure servers with restricted access
- We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your meal selection data may be shared with:
- Meal service administrators for order processing
- Service providers who assist in app functionality (e.g., Firebase, notification services)

These parties are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Update or correct your meal preferences
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Opt-out of push notifications
- Withdraw consent for data processing

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending you a notification if changes are material

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:
- **Firebase:** For authentication, data storage, and cloud functions
- **Expo:** For app development and deployment
- Push notification services

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

## Compliance

This app complies with:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents
- Apple App Store and Google Play Store privacy requirements

## Data Retention

- Active user data is retained while your account is active
- Meal selection history may be retained for administrative purposes
- You may request data deletion by contacting us or deleting your account
- Deleted data is removed from our systems within 30 days

## Cookies and Tracking

Our app does not use cookies. We may collect anonymized usage statistics to improve app performance.

By using our app, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
